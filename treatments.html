THEATRE
  • Our theatre is equipped with modern E.N.T equipment.
  • Our anaesthetic machine and multiparameter patient monitor have safety devices.
  • Our patient monitor is a full modular capable of monitoring, recording and storing patient data during operation. It has ability for complete gas analysis system (Oxygen, carbon dioxide & anaesthetic agents) contained within the sensor heads ( modules).
  • The ETCO2 and AG module monitor provide valuable information about CO2 and anaesthetic gas agents during anaesthesia.

Safety is one of our core values at A-Ilupeju E.N.T Hospital.

C-MAC Pocket Monitor VIDEO LARYNGOSCOPE

It is used in tracheal intubation for surgical procedures during general anesthesia in our hospital. It is one of the most recent advancements in endotracheal intubation as compared to the conventional Macintosh laryngoscopy where the anesthesiologist has a narrow view of the airway structures, which can be further obscured during attempts to pass the endotracheal tube and sometimes the endotracheal tube may slip into the esophagus.

The C-MAC Pocket monitor VIDEO LARYNGOSCOPE allows for an enlarged video image of airway structures during the process of anesthesia induction thereby allowing for accurate intubation of the larynx and trachea. Again, it is all about patient safety, which is one of our core values.
