About the founder
Dr. Adedji Taiwo

Founder & Chief ENT Surgeon | Pioneer in Stitch-less Ear Surgery

With over 20 years of surgical excellence, Dr. Adedeji Taiwo stands as one of Nigeria's most respected ENT specialists.

Dr. Adedeji has authored multiple research papers focusing on ENT-related conditions such as sinusitis, hearing loss, otitis media, and the management of foreign bodies in the ear and nasal passages. His work has been recognized in both clinical and academic circles, reflecting his dedication to improving ENT care and advancing medical knowledge in Nigeria.

Our Mission

To Bring Sense to every healing and bring harmony to every sense

Ear Treatment

We offer comprehensive diagnosis and treatment for a wide range of ear-related conditions, including hearing loss, tinnitus, ear infections, eardrum perforations, and congenital ear abnormalities. Our team is skilled in advanced procedures such as microscopic ear surgery, cochlear implantation, and techniques designed to promote faster, more comfortable recovery.

Nose Treatment

We specialize in diagnosing and managing a wide spectrum of nasal conditions from chronic sinusitis, nasal obstruction, and allergies to complex issues like deviated septum, and nasal deformities. Our approach combines advanced imaging and endoscopic evaluations . We offer both medical therapy and minimally invasive procedures, like functional endoscopic sinus surgery (FESS) and septoplasty , ensuring effective results.

Ear Treatment

Our throat care services address everything from common conditions like sore throat, tonsillitis, and voice changes to more complex concerns such as swallowing disorders, vocal cord paralysis, and obstructive sleep apnea. We use modern diagnostic tools including laryngoscopy and sleep studies to ensure accurate evaluation. Treatment options range from conservative management to advanced surgical procedures focused on restoring comfort and quality sleep.
